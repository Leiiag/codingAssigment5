This recipe is a classic layered lasagna with meat sauce, pasta, and melted cheese.

Rating & Conversion

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Serves: 8 people

Conversion: 1 cup = 16 tablespoons

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning

Equipment

  • Large pot
  • Skillet
  • Mixing bowl
  • 9x13 baking dish
  • Foil
  • Oven mitts

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Cook noodles according to package directions.
  3. Brown the meat in a skillet.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, meat, and cheese.
  5. Bake covered for 25 minutes.
  6. Remove foil and bake 10 more minutes.
